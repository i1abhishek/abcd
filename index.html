IEEE VNIT

10/09/2018

IEEE VNIT Student Branch conducted its first workshop on Basic Electronics on the 9th of September 2018. In this workshop, students learnt basics concepts of electronics and about components like diodes, transistors, logic gates, op-amps, LDR, 555 Timer IC etc. They also got hands-on experience on various electronic components & circuits like touch sensor using transistor, automatic street light control system using LDR & transistors, etc.
